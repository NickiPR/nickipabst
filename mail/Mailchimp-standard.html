Our 2021 season is open for booking from June 26 to END DATE.
We can’t wait to see you this summer at Denmark’s smallest campsite: a simple and sustainable approach to living with nature nestled in the dunes of Balka Strand, Bornholm.

Our luxurious tents are outfitted with everything you need: a comfortable bed, soft linens, warm blankets, in-tent power, and bath towels. Our organic cafe has delicious breakfast, lunch and snacks and our bonfire is waiting to welcome you.

We have twelve tents and three pricing options. Glamp in the Dunes in secluded comfort within view of the ocean, or choose our Classic Glamping closer to the heartbeat of camp. For couples, we offer Cozy Glamping — the same amenities in a tent with a smaller footprint. Booking is only possible via Eco Beach Camp’s website. Please review our Booking Conditions and FAQ before you reserve.
